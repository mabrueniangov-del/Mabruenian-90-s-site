Government Structure

The government of Mabruenia is a constitutional monarchy with a parliamentary system.

The Crown serves as the ceremonial head of state, while the government is run by elected officials and appointed ministers.

The legislative branch is the Parliament, which consists of the House of Representatives and the House of Lords.

The executive branch is headed by the Chancellor, who is appointed by the Crown and voted on by the people, and is responsible for running the government and implementing policies.

The judiciary is independent and is responsible for interpreting the laws of Mabruenia and ensuring justice is served.

Overall, the government of Mabruenia is designed to balance the power of the Crown with the will of the people, while ensuring that justice and fairness are upheld.
